Iraq: Quagmire or Stunning Success?
It's an odd sort of world when 'quagmire' and 'stunning success' describe the same war, which is today just two weeks old and over two hundred miles long. Neither is really an entirely correct description for the war. Coalition forces have traversed over two hundred miles, crushed elite Republican Guard units, and liberated thousands of Iraqis, even when the liberators were met with pointed weapons. It has been a conflict that has taken over a hundred American and British heroes from their families, from their friends, and from their country. ....Full story.....by William E. Hudson
