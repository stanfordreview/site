Smoke Signals
With countless flyers around campus advertising the need for RoHo's and exhorting students to the "Fundamental Standard," everyone is reminded that in one week Stanford will be overrun with hundreds of admits to the class of 2006. So for current students and ProFros alike, here are two takes on a few of the sights to look out for during Admit Weekend:
The quad, bathed in sunlight Not quite as remarkable as the main quad, bathed in the light of the first full moon of the school year.
The fountains The beautiful crystal-clear fountains that for one weekend...will all be running!
The melodious cadence of the Hoover's bells The eerie and completely random music that *supposedly* hails from the home of the Hoover Institution.
EANABs The Equally Attractive, Non-Alcoholic Beverages will make their highly anticipated annual appearance.
The oh-so-informative panels and presentations Don't worry if you miss one! It will be offered again during Orientation, Parents' Weekend, and again next year.
