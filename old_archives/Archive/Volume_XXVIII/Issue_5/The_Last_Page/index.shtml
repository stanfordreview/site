The Last Page
Special ASSU Election Report
The ASSU elections were held earlier this month, spawning great excitement and a complete and utter lack of voter apathy. Everybody cared very deeply about these elections. EVERYBODY.

Despite the fact that the competitive candidates were all proud tree-hugging liberals, estimates put the number of redwoods killed by the omnipresent campaign fliers at "several forests." One candidate noted, "Don't tell anyone, but there's only one thing I consider more important than the environment's needs--my needs." When prompted to elaborate, Gore's spokesperson said he was unavailable for comment. Students were quick to note their enthusiasm for the week's events. As their comments demonstrate, the fanfare of the ASSU Election lured thousands of young minds into the excitement of the frenzied race for the coveted position of "ASSU President."

The Student Voice*
"Personally, I think we need to see more dinosaurs in this process. Why don't we elect a new dinosaur each year as the unofficial ASSU Chief? And then we'd put up pictures of him or her on all ASSU fliers. I would vote for the Tyrannosaurus Rex, because he could eat Os*fsky in one bite." ~ mathematics major.

"Wait. There was an actual election? I thought it was some kind of elaborate parody." ~anonymous

"I was going to the bathroom the other day, and I walked into a stall, and there he was, staring at me from one of those d**m pieces of paper. I mean, holy sh*t, I already see these stupid fliers everywhere I go on campus. Does he have to f*ing follow me into the restroom, too?" ~ male athlete.

"Election? I thought that awful man was proposing to the poor girl whose name I saw on all those fliers." ~ unaware pre-med student.

"These fliers keep talking about problems none of us have. I don't have a transportation problem. Do you? Why doesn't someone do something about the ugly election fliers everywhere; there's a problem that I'd like solved." ~ stressed poli-sci major.

"Isn't it a little early to be asking about that? It doesn't start until November. Personally, though, I think Bush is going to lose." ~ cheerful psychology major.

"Who is 'Benson Arimoto' whose name you place everywhere? I do not understand this land, and your practice to put paper all over to cover the beautiful campus." ~Apu**

"If they see my name enough, they'll have to vote for me! They'll dream of me in their repose, and in their waking hours my name shall be their only deliberation. The outcome is predestined. Victory is mine!" ~ anonymous

*one or more of these quotes may have been fabricated by the author.
**Apu is an extremely intelligent, diligent student, who comes from a culture rich in art and history that deserves great respect. Also, he runs the Kwik-E-Mart.
