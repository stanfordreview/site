Opinion
Counterpoint: The Essence of Moral Reason
by Alex Rawls
Comrade

The key to moral reason is to start with your presumptions and look for the most defensible way to support them. Only in this way can your true self--your first impressions--remain untainted by all the misleading influences that abound in the world. For instance, as a generous human being, I naturally love all animals, and just as I wouldn't eat a grandparent who I love, neither would I eat an animal. "I love you cattle" I wave out the window.

A friend points out that if people didn't eat cattle, no one would raise cattle. Instead of thriving above all other animal species on the planet, none of these cattle would exist. But I have a devastating response: "I don't think of it that way." All she could do was throw up her hands. I won.

The most defensible position of all, you see, is to only care what is defensible to you. Within the sanctity of your own mind, you can defend whatever you want simply by ignoring what assails it. Isn't this the tower of moral strength that has been celebrated throughout history? Tortured by captors, the hero's mind remains his own. By asserting sovereign power over the mind, every sling and arrow can be kept from the self.

The same principle applies to reasons and arguments, should they assail one's presumptions. Through simple sovereign control of mind, opposing reasons can simply be deleted from one's reality. What is the alternative? Are you to stop loving animals? Are you to eat your grandparents? Filter out then, whatever contrary thinking any might offer. Deny it access to your mind as you would deny a torturer's attempt to pervert your will. Go even further. Turn the tables.

Suppose you hear someone speculate about the influence of genes on the human being. Call him a racist! If genes have no effect on the human being then nothing negative can ever be attributed to race and racism becomes impossible. Hence it is the belief that genes can affect the human being that is the cause of all racism. No one should be allowed to get away with that! Like you, the racist may have learned to ignore contrary understanding. Don't let him. Scream "racist" in public and don't let up. Any attempt at answer, just shout "racist" again and again. Can his moral capacity to shut out all but his own mind possibly match yours? Of course not. He is your inferior. Challenge him to a test of wills and watch him buckle under your onslaught. Thus must the moral do battle with the immoral, and defeat them.
