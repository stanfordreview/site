The Man Who Made Conservatism Hip
Jonah Goldberg
Jonah Goldberg is the editor of the National Review Online (NRO), the hugely successful website of the National Review. NRO started as his column in September 1998, and has since grown to 2.5 million hits and 210,000 unique visitors a month. While other online magazines like Salon.com are struggling, NRO -- already large -- is thriving and expanding, mostly because of its younger reader base which is attracted by pop culture references and hooked by the writing.
