The Last Page
Politically Incorrect Horoscopes
Capricorn (Dec 21 - Jan 19)
Hold gratuitous hunger strikes at night in the middle of Highway 101. Promote a Hands Across the Mojave. Start a letter-writing campaign to free your favorite white-collor criminal. Include your resume. 		Cancer (Jun 21 - Jul 22)
Be prepared for nothing fun to happen on campus for the remainder of the school year. Whoever told you college was a good time--filled with keg parties and cheery co-eds--graduated a long, long time ago. We hate people like that. We are here to study. If you are looking for a good time try swing dancing.
Aquarius (Jan 20 - Feb 19)
Your salary will be raised to $117,000 per year. Yor output of scholarly articles will fall drastically. In fact, who cares about writing things no one reads or cares about. You will not have any new ideas for the next thirty years. 		Leo (Jul 23 - Aug 22)
you can't keep your hands off fried chicken. You feebly attempt to resist your ultimate desire but fail miserably, soiling your name and embarrassing your family. Sinisterly cunning and deluded by images of grandeur, you also sneak behind your wife's back to carry out your love affair with French fries and fast food chains, though you forcefully decree before the media that you did not have any inappropriate relationships. Claim that you never touched "that chicken breast." Eventually, you will be removed from office, convicted for abuse of power, and then catapulted into the Arctic Ocean.
Pisces (Feb 20 - Mar 20)
You are a Daily columnist. You are boring without being serious. Compare the crisis in Kosovo to the extra-large smoothie offered at Jamba Juice. Find a relationship between NATO and the Stanford Post Office. Insert yourself and your best friend into a column about Slobodan Milosevic. Applaud everything the university has ever done. 		Virgo (Aug 23 - Sep 22)
You are a professor. You have not left campus since 1974. You clench your fist at the Hoover Institution every day. Rich Republicans, unlike rich Democrats, ought not to be tolerated on a college campus. Kick Hoover fellows off campus--and into the U.S. Congress.
Aries (Mar 21 - Apr 19)
You "invented" the Internet. You like the environment and your wife Tipper, too. Penniless Buddhist monks will leave $500,000 in cash for you underneath a pew. 		Libra (Sep 23 - Oct 22)
Satan reveres you as a god. You enjoy torturing small furry animals and those over whom you have much power. You are ruthless, unyielding, unkind. You thrive on the agony of others. Most teaching assistants are Libras.
Taurus (Apr 20 - May 20)
Live carefree and dangerously--vote Democrat. Put two bumper stickers on your SUV. Protest everything: Paper, plastic, tenure, tuna, life, grapes, and a decent education. Invest in the future--get your child started off with a healthy trust fund. 		Scorpio (Oct 23 - Nov 21)
You will be nominated to the board of a major university. Immediately raise tuition. Instigate a competition with rival universities over who has more robots on campus. Use the endowment to replace professors with state-of-the-art robots. Watch your school move up in the U.S. News and World Report's annual rankings. Give yourself a raise!
Gemini (May 21 - Jun 20)
Avoid classes at all costs. Close your mind to imperialistic Western academia and read Rigoberta Menchu. Withdraw from Stanford, sell everything to Marxist guerrilla outfits in South America such as the Review. Move to Columbia, and make peace with your inner Republican. Do exactly the opposite of what comes naturally. 		Sagittarius (Nov 22 - Dec 20)
You just read an entire Stanford Review. Your parents have been notified and the proper authorities have been called. Apologize profusely and say that you were on your way to recycling it when another side-splitting back page caught your eye. Never let it happen again until next issue.

You have Peter Watts, Mike Toth, and Dan Parker to blame for this back page. Page last modified on Wednesday, 01-Mar-2006 23:52:17 MST.
