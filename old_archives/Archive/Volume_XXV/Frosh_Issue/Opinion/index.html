What Student Union?
Memorial Union, Stanford's only student union, currently contains Jamba Juice, Tresidder Express, Pulse, Fitness Center, three restaurants, and two banks. Some students consider it indispensable for those otherwise forced to use off-campus facilities and face the inflated prices of the Bay Area. Compared to the student unions at other universities, however, which often serve as student social centers, late-night hangouts, and favorite lunch and dinner destinations, Tresidder seems far less valuable. .....Full story in Opinion.....by Daniel Maeng
