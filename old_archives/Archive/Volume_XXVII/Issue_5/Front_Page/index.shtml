ASSU Funded by Endowment, Enterprise
Ever wonder what happened to the $70 fee that ASSU charged you at the beginning of the quarter? Most students believe it goes toward supporting the ASSU itself, but this is not the case. Rather, it funds a number of student groups annually selected in spring quarter special-fee elections. Stanford Student Enterprises, a collection of student-run businesses, funds the ASSU itself. "If you can grasp this subtlety and understand that no student money supports the ASSU," says Ross Davisson, ASSU Financial Manager and President of SSE, "you will know something that not many students do." He explains, "The ASSU is self-funded." ....Full story.....by Kevin McCaffrey and Katie Kollitz
