Update
The Stanford Review has updated the following two stories as of Tuesday, September 25, at 11:45 a.m. PST.

Stanford Community Responds to Terrorism
The Stanford community on campus and around the United States and the world responded to Tuesday's terrorist acts with outrage at the perpetrators, compassion for the victims, and prayers for every individual involved.

The Stanford community on campus and around the United States and the world responded to Tuesday's terrorist acts with outrage at the violence, compassion for the victims, and prayers for every individual involved.

Stanford students working and living in Washington, D.C. and New York City experienced firsthand the day's events as they unfolded. The Career Development Center was unavailable for comment on the number of Stanford students working in the two cities this summer. A school like Stanford where Economics and Political Science are among the most popular majors would expect to have many students interning in the nation's economic and political capitals, as many have called the two cities in the wake of the attacks.....Full story
