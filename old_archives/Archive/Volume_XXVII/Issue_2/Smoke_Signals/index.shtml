Smoke Signals
Students pay $38,000 a year for their world-class Stanford education. Here are the Chief's picks for the classes that students will remember, for better or for worse:
Introduction to Economics (Econ 1): Michael Boskin taught Messrs. Bush and Reagan, and now he's teaching you.
Peace Studies (Poli Sci 133): As if the campus pacifists need any more help.
Frontiers of Chemical Science (Chem 32): Dashing freshmen pre-med hopes year after year.
Images of Women in French Cinema (French 192B): 2 GER's. Pornography. 'Nuff said.
Social Dance I (Drama 46): A great chance to meet that perfect grad student. Too bad the never-ending rotations makes him/her gone in sixty seconds.

Compiled by: Julie Park and Ryan Wisnesky.
