Letter to the Editor
America, Stand Strong
The university professors and other "pacifists" who blame the September 11 terrorism on past policies and actions of the United States are way out in "left field." Let's remind them of the many wonderful things our great country has done for the world.

1. Fought a terrible civil war to abolish slavery.

2. Helped save the world from dictatorships and communism in two world wars and then gave back all the captured territories.

3. Bankrolled Europe after WWII with the Marshall Plan.

4. Wrote the present Japanese constitution, bringing democracy and economic wealth to a former enemy.

5. Rescued South Korea from communism and helped them become an economic powerhouse.

6. We allow more immigration than all other nations combined and have made huge advances in eliminating racism.

Of course the Gulf War was fought to assure the supply of oil for the world economy and that involved assuring the freedom of both Kuwait and Saudi Arabia. We should apologize for that? Our own domestic critics seem not to recognize or appreciate our freedoms or our standard of living. They look for and celebrate all the negatives. They are poor souls who cannot celebrate and enjoys the wonders of the U.S.A. Poor babies!

Morgan Sanborn
San Clemente, CA
