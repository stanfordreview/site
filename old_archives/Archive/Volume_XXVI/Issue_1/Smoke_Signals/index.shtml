Smoke Signals
After Ben Stein's somewhat poorly publicized visit last Autumn, the Review has not been happy with the speakers on campus recently. Who would we like to see? Here are a few suggestions for the Spring:
Alan Keyes: The Review knows where students' loyalities lie.
Marc Rich: Having trouble with your tax forms? Learn all the shortcuts.
Pat Buchanan: Learn how to fail at politics.
John Rouer: Hopefully, MemAud won't fill past capacity for this one.
Jesse Jackson: Hey! Ho! Family values have got to go!
