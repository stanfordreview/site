Smoke Signals
It's business as usual on The Farm. The administration is implementing more intrusive policies, dorms are getting more crowded, parking is harder to find, and tuition is up. University officials almost didn't let Phi Kappa Psi light table candles at our semi-formal, but backed off when they realized 100 Alumni were present...we don't want to show the 'source of wealth' how lame our policies have become. Sure, blame caution on the lawyers...what do you think your poli-sci major is encouraging? Hard work? Continuing...
Office of Student Activities Unless the Review writes a better Constitution, we've been told we'll be kicked out of our facilities. Better not have a 10th Amendment, is my guess.
Hennessy writes to the Daily Implying a moral equivalency between Baraka and Barak really showed his open mind--I'm glad we have such a caring President.
Amiri Baraka Perfectly justified righteous wrath is hard to come by these days with all the shades of gray lying around, and we thank Baraka for his help in finding someone we can all agree to hate.
ASSU's Good Judgment Despite being led by an economically--impaired liberal activist, they've refrained from passing an anti-war measure as they did in '90.
Alcohol Probation for Fraternities Everyone but Phi Kappa Psi is on alcohol probation. And their parties are invite-only! But wait...I'm in that fraternity. Phew.
