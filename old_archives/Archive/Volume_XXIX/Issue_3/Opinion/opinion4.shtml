Opinion
Bowling for Columbine: Ethnics => Murder?
by Alec Rawls
Senior Staff Writer

In Bowling for Columbine, anti-gun activist Michael Moore knocks on NRA President Charleton Heston's house. Heston invites him in to ask some questions and Moore starts badgering Heston with the imputation that America's high murder rate is due to our high rate of gun ownership. In fact, our unusually high murder rate has a well known explanation, which Heston tries to delicately mention: that it has a lot to do with our "mixed ethnicity."

Moore's reaction to this remark is pure demagoguery. In interviews about his movie, he takes Heston's "mixed ethnicity" comment as proof that the gun rights movement is driven by racism. ''I was shocked when he said that,'' Moore gushes: ''There's a racial thread that goes through this film and leads up to that moment. The underpinning of our collective fear is based on racism. So, for him to volunteer this . . . ''

But Heston is factually correct. Blacks, at 13 percent of the U.S. population, commit over half the murders . Compared to a country like Canada, America's higher murder rate (a little more than twice Canada's) is almost entirely explained by the violence of our much larger black population, which is only one part of our "mixed ethnicity."

Hispanics, a much larger part of the population than blacks, also have an above average crime rate. In particular, our high murder rate is NOT explained by America's tradition of gun rights, which has been thoroughly proven by riminologists to be a powerful deterrent to crime.

It was politically incorrect in the extreme for Heston to mention the factual explanation for most if not all of America's above average murder rate, but it was not racist. Facts cannot be racist. Suppressing facts, however, can be, and this is where those who are worried about racism should focus.

Facts are the starting point for solutions. Gun rights activists like Charlton Heston fight every day to get blacks their gun rights, so that they can defend themselves against the extraordinarily high rate of crime that they face. But it takes analysis, using all available light, to have confidence in this solution.

With blacks committing half the murders, can we really let them have guns? The fearful un-thought-out response is "no." That is the response that led the supposedly less-racist northern states to copy gun control from the south as blacks migrated north. But such fears quickly vanish from a mind that is open to reason and evidence, no matter how politically incorrect they may be. Yes blacks have six times the crime rate as whites, but that also means that six times as many blacks as whites have forfeited their gun rights by virtue of felony record. The bad guys get disarmed, regardless of race. Unless blacks are better at hiding vicious criminal tendencies than whites, which there is no reason to suppose, gun rights for those blacks who are not disqualified by criminal history will have just as powerful a net crime reducing effect as gun rights in general.

It doesn't matter whether the "ethnic" crime rate is more attributable to nature or nurture. The possibility that the bell-curve of inherent tendencies could be different from one race to another is irrelevant. All that matters is where a particular individual lies on the bell curve of humanity, post nature and nurture.

The P.C. mind never gets that far. It knows about black crime rates but cannot think or speak about these facts, leaving its fears unnamed, unexplored, irrational. Thus Heston, embracing the truth, is a champion of black rights, while Michael Moore, the politically correct clown, is just a bigot, an anti-gun bigot, whose darkly clouded mind roils with contempt for everybody's rights.
