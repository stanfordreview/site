Smoke Signals
Liberal Meetings abound, aside from the objectivist seminar with free copies of Atlas Shrugged, and one or two conservative speaker events. Hospital workers strike and get living wage offers, my cool four-square playing Computer Science professor is extremely anti-war, students at a talk about Amiri Baraka believe silly Hezbollah lies about the world, and environmental action students with--according to a liberal friend--"the collective intelligence of a small squirrel (a brown one, not the ninja black ones)" meet and talk politics. Ah, the joys of Stanford.
Pro-Suicide-Bomb Attorney Rejected Law School Dean Kathleen Sullivan announced she was rescinding the school's offer of a visiting mentorship to lawyer Lynne Stewart.
Environmental Action Seminar Held War is a form of racism, and labor activism can be a form of environmental policy? These are "liberal" issues, not "environmental" issues, kids.
The Daily's coverage of Nathan Peterson The Daily would like to inform you that Nate "The Rapist" was not charged. Probably because of insufficient evidence. He still might be a rapist. This isn't libel, it's FYI...yeah.
Stanford Daily conducts Survey about Iraq after Citing Review Survey Nice job guys, but "interviewing 50 students and getting 93 percent" citing a reason to attack Iraq means your math still isn't quite there.
Stanford offers $11.35 Living Wage to Workers Sigh. Liberals, liberals, everywhere. Oh well.
