Opinion
Conservative Argument Against War
Right now, it's hard to be someone who is for limited government and at the same time against the war on Iraq. I find myself agreeing with people I never agree with, and disagreeing with people who are usually on my side on most issues. Too many people assume that if you are for minimal government, you also have to be for the war. On the surface, this doesn't make much sense. It seems to follow naturally that someone who is for tax cuts and cuts in government spending would not want our government to go spend more money on a war. After all, war is the health of the state. If you want to cut back government, going to war is not the way to do it. War grows the government. When government grows, freedom most certainly does not. ....Full story.....by Jeffrey Lonsdale

Pro-War: Putting Hindsight and Foresight Together to Pressure Iraq
With the extent of the case presented by the Bush administration as to the imperative of forcing Saddam Hussein to surrender his weapons of mass destruction, it may seem tedious to continue belaboring the need to justify a potential war on Iraq. Unfortunately, recent media coverage of the British interview with Saddam Hussein published on February 4 and Secretary of State Colin Powell's case to the UN made on February 5 demonstrate that media sources -- and through them many Americans -- still don't perceive the essence of the problem. ....Full story.....by Piotr. Tax Exemptions for Investment Income are Anti-Natalist
The Bush administration is proposing two tax cuts that aim to exempt investment income from "double taxation." One would exempt dividend income from taxation, and the other would broaden the individual retirement account program to cover more types of individual savings in larger amounts. If either is passed, the effect will be a drastic shift of liquidity from young to old that will greatly depress child-bearing in America. Social Security is already waging war on the young. That burden is about to be doubled. Stand up and fight, kids. Your necks are on the block. ....Full story.....by Alec Rawls
